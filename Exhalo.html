Exhalo

Arduino • Poster Presentation • Healthcare

Collaborated with three others to meet with stakeholders and conduct surveys over the course of a year to learn about rural healthcare. Researched pre-existing healthcare solutions and pitfalls. Engaged in iterative engineering design to ideate a device that allows low-cost, portable, breath-based analysis for diabetes testing. Utilized Arduino to develop a prototype that can detect VOCs in breath. Presented my device at a poster showcase that was attended by over 150 industry professionals, stakeholders, and others affiliated with GT. Won "Instructors Choice award" and $800 of additional funding.
