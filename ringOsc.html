Custom Ring Oscillator

Chip Design • Analog • Circuit Design

For this project, I had to create a custom ring oscillator on Cadence Virtuoso given certain design specs. While I was required to meet these specs over the tt corner, I also tested over ss and ff corners. I did this project just as I was starting my first ever microelectronics course, so needless to say the learning curve was steep! Using online resources, I self-studied current-starved ring oscillators and the Cadence software. I had to start from the base components, designing, testing, and laying out a custom inverter and buffer before designing and laying out my 5-stage ring oscillator. Along the way I also utilized a testbench and analyzed maestro waveforms to ensure I met specs. (And yes, I laid out the testbench as well).
